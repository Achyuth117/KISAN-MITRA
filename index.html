The Challenge

The Traditional Supply Chain is Broken

Agriculture forms the backbone of our economy, yet those who produce our food face the most significant hurdles.

15-20% Income Loss

Farmers lose a massive percentage of their rightful income to multiple layers of intermediaries and middlemen.

30-40% Crop Loss

Due to poor logistics, fragmented supply chains, and lack of immediate buyers, perishable crops are wasted.

The Solution

Direct & Transparent Access

Kisan Mitra replaces the fragmented chain with a single, highly efficient digital bridge connecting the farm gate to the buyer's warehouse.

WhatsApp-Based Platform

Designed for users with low digital literacy. No complex apps to download or maintain for farmers.

Direct Connection

Directly matches farmer produce with verified institutional buyers and food processors.

Transparent Pricing

Clear, upfront pricing with no hidden commissions. Both parties see exactly what is paid and received.
