How Kisan Mitra Works

A seamless digital handshake between rural farmers and institutional buyers.

1. Farmer Sends WhatsApp

The farmer sends a simple "Hi" to our verified WhatsApp business number.

2. Bot Collects Data

Our intelligent chatbot asks for crop details: Type, Quantity, and expected Price.

3. System Creates Listing

The system automatically sanitizes the data and ports it to the active marketplace.

4. Admin Verification

Kisan Mitra admins verify the listing for quality control and approve it.

5. Buyer Browses & Offers

Institutional buyers interact with the platform, filtering crops and placing direct orders.

6. Pickup Coordination

Once matched, logistics are dispatched directly to the farm gate.

7. Secure Payment

Funds are deposited directly into the farmer's linked bank account upon receipt of goods.
