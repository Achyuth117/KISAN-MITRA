Our Origin Story

Building a resilient platform to shield those who feed the nation.

The Vision

To democratize tech for the rural economy

For far too long, technological advancements have bypassed the very foundation of our nation: our farmers. Intermediaries swallowed their profits, logistics destroyed their perishables, and opacity ruled the markets.

Kisan Mitra was founded to change this dynamic via an interface every farmer uses effortlessly—WhatsApp. By abstracting complex B2B marketplace mechanics from the end farmer, we built the ultimate Direct-to-Buyer platform.
